About ArchiWiz

ArchiWiz - The experts in BIM Architecture and Visualization

ArchiWiz is a Florida, USA based BIM Architecture and Visualization company providing BIM modeling, architectural visualization, remote BIM management solutions for residential and commercial customers. We use CAD and PDF drawings to provide BIM solutions, clash detection, MIR(RFI) and content creation.

ArchiWiz offers BIM services for project coordination, asset management, reduction of risk, logistical planning, optimization of the costs and other activities for building projects. We specialize in Architectural, Structural and MEP Modeling including 3D Modeling, 4D Modeling, BIM Implementation and BIM Modeling Services.

Throughout the project life cycle, the ArchiWiz consulting team works as your project concierge and ensures that BIM technologies are efficiently integrated using best practices from the industry. The services of ArchiWiz BIM covers all elements of planning, design, construction, and operations and maintenance which make design and construction models, operations and maintenance more attractive, efficient, and effective.

Our Services include

  • Takeoffs - CMU, Flooring, framing, windows
  • Clash detection including MEP
  • Structural Revit 3D modeling
  • Rendering
  • As built verification, modeling, and drawings
  • 360 annotated tours and tours
  • Laser scanning of construction site, underground MEP, framing rough MEP,and final MEP & dry walls
  • 2D annotation drawings for subcontractors and other usages
  • Home Automation design and modeling
  • Yard design and layout
